Organ Donation Facts

  • One donor can save up to 8 lives.
  • Every 10 minutes, someone is added to the transplant waiting list.
  • Organs that can be donated include the heart, kidneys, liver, lungs, pancreas, and intestines.
  • Tissues such as corneas, skin, heart valves, and bones can also be donated.

Common Myths and Truths

  • Myth: Doctors won’t try to save my life if I’m an organ donor.
    Truth: Saving your life is always the top priority of medical professionals.
  • Myth: I’m too old to donate.
    Truth: There’s no age limit to organ donation.
  • Myth: Organ donation costs money.
    Truth: There is no cost to the donor’s family.

Why It Matters

In India, thousands die every year waiting for an organ transplant. By pledging your organs, you become a beacon of hope and a symbol of compassion. Your simple act today could be the miracle someone is praying for tomorrow.
